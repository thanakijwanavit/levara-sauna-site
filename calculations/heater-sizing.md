# Heater Sizing Calculations

## Electric Sauna (Traditional)

### Room Parameters
- **Net dimensions:** 1.90m × 2.31m × 2.25m (dropped ceiling)
- **Volume:** ~9.87 m³
- **Target temperature:** 75-90°C

### Sizing Factors

| Factor | Adjustment |
|--------|------------|
| Base volume | 9.87 m³ |
| Glass door (8mm) | +1.0 m³ per m² glass |
| Connecting door (glass) | +1.0 m³ per m² glass |
| Insulation quality | ±10-20% |
| Climate (ambient temp) | ±10% |

### Estimated Effective Volume
- Base: 9.87 m³
- Glass door (~1.6 m²): +1.6 m³
- Connecting door (~1.6 m²): +1.6 m³
- **Effective volume:** ~13 m³

### Heater Power Recommendation
- **Rule of thumb:** 1 kW per 1.0-1.5 m³
- **Calculated range:** 8.7-13 kW
- **Recommended:** **8-10 kW** class heater

### Notes
- Final sizing per heater manufacturer's calculator
- Account for actual insulation R-value
- Consider 3-phase power for 9+ kW heaters

## Infrared Sauna

### Room Parameters
- **Net dimensions:** 1.90m × 2.31m × 2.25m (dropped ceiling)
- **Volume:** ~9.87 m³
- **Target temperature:** 45-60°C

### Panel Configuration
Infrared saunas sized by body coverage, not air volume.

| Position | Panel Size | Power |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Back wall (bench) | Full length | ~1.0-1.5 kW |
| Side panels | Per layout | ~0.5-1.0 kW each |
| Calf/foot level | Optional | ~0.3-0.5 kW |

### Total Power Recommendation
- **Typical range:** 2.0-3.5 kW total
- **Recommended:** **2.5-3.0 kW** for this room size

### Notes
- Carbon panels: broader coverage, gentler heat
- Ceramic panels: more intense, focused heat
- Position panels for even body exposure
