# Sauna Materials Spec (Traditional Sauna Only)

## Engineered wood (structure/backing + **floor = engineered wood only**)

* **Plywood (exterior/WBP-grade)** — METROPLY (producer): [https://www.metroply.com/plywood](https://www.metroply.com/plywood)
* **OSB (Oriented Strand Board)** — Vanachai Group (producer): [https://www.vanachai.com/](https://www.vanachai.com/)

## Wood studs (framing)

* **Kiln-dried structural timber for studs** — Thaweephan (reference): [https://www.thaweephanwood.com/](https://www.thaweephanwood.com/)

## Insulation (glass wool)

* **Glass mineral wool insulation** — Knauf Insulation: [https://www.knaufinsulation.com/what-we-do/insulation-range/glass-mineral-wool](https://www.knaufinsulation.com/what-we-do/insulation-range/glass-mineral-wool)

## Vapor barrier (**aluminum foil sauna barrier**) — 2 choices

* **Aluminum foil + kraft sauna vapor barrier (roll)** — Sun Valley Saunas: [https://sunvalleysaunas.com/products/steam-sauna-living-vapor-barrier](https://sunvalleysaunas.com/products/steam-sauna-living-vapor-barrier)
* **Heat-proof aluminum vapor barrier paper (sauna use; Finland-made)** — Oceanic Saunas: [https://www.oceanicsaunas.com/product/vapour-barrier-paper/](https://www.oceanicsaunas.com/product/vapour-barrier-paper/)

## Real wood (final interior lining layer: walls/ceiling/benches) — 5 choices

1. **Hinoki (Japanese cypress)** — species reference: [https://www.j-wood.org/en/tree-species/hinoki/](https://www.j-wood.org/en/tree-species/hinoki/)
2. **Western Red Cedar** — species reference: [https://www.wood-database.com/western-red-cedar/](https://www.wood-database.com/western-red-cedar/)
3. **Aspen** — species reference: [https://www.wood-database.com/european-aspen/](https://www.wood-database.com/european-aspen/)
4. **Nordic Spruce (Norway spruce)** — species reference: [https://www.wood-database.com/norway-spruce/](https://www.wood-database.com/norway-spruce/)
5. **ThermoWood (thermally modified wood)** — ThermoWood Association: [https://www.thermowood.fi/](https://www.thermowood.fi/)
