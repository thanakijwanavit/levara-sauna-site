# Floor Specification

## Vestibule

### Assembly (Bottom → Top)
1. Structural floor
2. Waterproof membrane (liquid-applied or sheet)
3. Tile or microcement finish

### Drainage
- **Linear drain** recommended
- Slope floor minimum 1% toward drain
- Stainless steel or tile-insert drain cover

### Optional Enhancement
- Cold rinse shower connection (compact wall-mounted)
- Requires additional drain capacity

---

## Saunas (Both Rooms)

### Assembly (Bottom → Top)
1. Structural floor
2. Waterproof membrane
3. Non-slip tile or microcement
4. **Removable hinoki duckboards** (on top)

### Duckboard Specification
- Hinoki wood, 20mm slats
- Stainless steel or hidden fasteners
- Removable for cleaning and maintenance
- Allow 10mm gaps between slats for drainage

### Notes
- Duckboards provide comfort and allow water/sweat to drain
- Easy removal for regular cleaning of tile substrate
- Replace duckboards as needed (expected 5–10 year lifespan)
