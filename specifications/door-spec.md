## All Sauna Doors

### Dimensions
- **Clear opening:** 800mm (minimum 700mm)
- **Height:** 2000mm

### Swing Direction
- **All doors swing outward** (away from sauna interior)
- Critical for safety egress

### Construction Options

#### Option A: Glass Door
- Tempered glass, 8–10mm thick
- Wood frame (hinoki or similar)
- Full glass or glass with wood rails

#### Option B: Wood Door
- Insulated sauna-rated wood construction
- Vision panel (tempered glass insert)
- Solid wood or veneered core

### Hardware
- Hinges: Sauna-rated (stainless steel, 3 per door)
- Latch: Magnetic catch only
- **No keyed locks** (safety requirement)
- Handle: Wood or low-conductivity material

### Seals
- Silicone or high-temp rubber gaskets
- Maintain seal under heat expansion
- Adjustable threshold if required
