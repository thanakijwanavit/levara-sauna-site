# Hinoki Wood Specification

## General

Japanese Hinoki (Chamaecyparis obtusa) for all interior sauna finishes.

## Applications

| Location | Thickness | Profile |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Wall/Ceiling Cladding | 12–15mm | Tongue & Groove |
| Benches | 25–30mm | Planed smooth |
| Backrests | 20–25mm | Planed smooth |
| Duckboards | 20mm | Slatted |

## Performance Requirements

- **Grade:** Clear or select grade, minimal knots
- **Moisture Content:** 8–12% at installation
- **Finish:** No varnish, lacquer, or sealants in sauna zones
- **Fasteners:** Stainless steel, hidden where possible

## Alternative (Benches)

Abachi (Triplochiton scleroxylon) may be substituted for bench surfaces where lower surface temperature is preferred.

## Notes

- Hinoki selected for natural antimicrobial properties and aromatic qualities
- Allow material to acclimate on-site for minimum 48 hours before installation
